Greenhouse

In your green house, your main priority is having space to store your growing plants, the materials you need to grow your plants (soil, watering cans, growbeds, etc.) and seeds.

Pantry

All you need for a pantry is a 10'x8'x8' space, 6"x6"x10'(x8) boards, 6"x6"x5' (x4) boards, construct it as you will. In this pantry it'll be a good idea to the shelves a foot apart to store gallon containers. These gallon containers could be filled with a variety of things. Though come to think of it you can also store macien jars

A Bunker

In this bunker, you should have an armory, a treasury, a locker room and a panic room.

On The Road Survival

There is always a chance of having to travel to find your Oasis. Here is what you'll need to survive on the road. Personally, I like to travel light. I usually travel with several lighters, a hammock with a space blanket
