Defining What Health Is

The overall definition of health is the condition, or state, of something or someone is in. Lets define what a healthy human is. If you may, imagine the human body as a machine, a.k.a. human anatomy and phisology. Though, don't forget it's about mental and psychological health too. Your mental and psychological health is also determined by how you can cope with external and internal influences, social situations and stressors.

Why Be Healthy?

For a lot of people, it's having a socially acceptable image, for some it's for self image, for others it's just to have a healthy offspring. On a philisophical stand point it's whatever you wish your health to be. On a physical stand point, it'll give you optimal preformance from all of your organs.

Health in a survival situation is vital if you either: a)you want to get back to civilization, or b)you want to find people to start a commune with, or c)if you just want to simply stay on the road. Your health in the case means that your overall state should be so that you can fight if neccesary, run if you have to, carry your goods and products and be able to have a clear mind.
