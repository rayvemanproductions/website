50 Cent

Born as Curtis James Jackson III on July 6, 1975. He's an American Rapper, singer, entreprenuer, investor, and actor. Mr. Jackson founded G-Unit records in 2003 with his associates Young Buck, Lloyd Banks and Tony Yayo. He has won several awards, including: a Grammy Award, 13 Billboard Music Awards, 6 World Music Awards, 3 American Music Awards, and 4 BET Awards.
