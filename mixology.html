Broken Leg

1 Part Jim Beam Bourbon Whiskey
2 Part Hot Apple Cider
Combine ingredients in a large mug. Garnish with lemon Wedge and cinnamon stick.

Mexican Mule

1 part Sauza 901 Tequila
2 part Ginger Beer
1/2 Lime
Add ingredients and juice from half a lime over ice. Garnish with a fresh lime wedge.

Sauza-Rita

1 can Sauza Blue Silver Tequila
1 bottle Light Beer
1 can Frozen Lemonade
1 can Water
Pour limeade and beer into a pitcher with ice. Fill limeade can with Sauza Blue Silver and pour into pitcher. Finally, add a can of water, stir and enjoy. If Sauza blue Silver is not available, try Sauza Silver.
