Personal Projects

Here are some options for when you meditate.

How is your morality? Do you have any self-desipline? Can you improve? Is your logic and reasoning in order? Are you compassionate towards your loved one? Are you a good friend? Are you a good guest? How are your manners? How is your attendence? How are you at controlling your emotions? Do you got a life plan? Write about different races and why racism is unimportant? Write about different cultures? How is your time management? How is your budgeting? Write about goals and knocking them out. Do you take passion in what you do?
