Spirituality is the quality or state of being concerned with religion or religious matters or the quality or state of being spiritual.

Religion is the belief in a god or in a group of gods, usually in an organized system of beliefs, ceremonies and rules. It might also be an interest, a belief, or an activity that is very important to a person or group.
